Agua de Pinole

Agua de pinole is a popular refreshing mexican drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of pinole
  • 1 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • 1 litres of water
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Vanilla essence

Preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients in a jar and mix until the sugar is disolved
  2. Store the mix in the refrigerator until it is cold
  3. Serve and enjoy
