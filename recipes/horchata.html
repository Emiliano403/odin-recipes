Agua de Horchata

Agua de horchata is a popular refreshing mexican drink.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of condensed milk
  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 cup of evaporated milk
  • 2 litres of water
  • 1 piece of cinammon
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  • Vanilla essence

Preparation

  1. Clean the rice
  2. Heat the water up till boiling point.
  3. Add the rice and stop heating
  4. After one hour blend the mixture using a blender
  5. Add the rest of the ingredients
